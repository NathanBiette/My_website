Overview

Aungmented is an animated short created by 3 persons about a mercenary agent sent on a mission to retrieve critical data stored on a floppy disk. It is set in a cyberpunk universe and has been rendered in Maya with Arnold renderer.

Creation

The main source of inspiration for our animated-short was the announcement trailer for Cyberpunk 2077 (2013). After a brainstorming session, we decided to go for the cyberpunk aesthetic because it would enable us to mix lots of different sci-fi assets without worrying too much about the cohesion of the different pieces.

With no previous experience in modelling, texturing or animation, we decided to build a unique room. We would tell a story by moving the camera around, slowly unveiling plot elements and details.
Music would be important, driving the discovery and the action.
We designed multiple versions of the bar. This part was tricky, as we adapted the layout of the room to make it possible to unveil elements one by one without showing too much of other parts. We still made sure the layout of the bar would make sense from a practical point-of-view.
In parallel, I draw the story-board, detailing each camera shot, angle, and moves.
